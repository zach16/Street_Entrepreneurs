About Street Entrepreneurs

Street Entrepreneurs or famously known as Street Vendors is an important parcel in the integration into the perspective of the micro economy concerning the urban populace. The participation of the Street Entrepreneurs is pinnacle to the livelihood of the individuals or families that dwell in their daily earning or them to extract any level of remuneration from the offering of various products at an acceptable price and accessible by the location. Street Entrepreneurs being an informal sector derived from the inability for formal employment and the reasoning to stay apart from poverty.

The Community

Street Entrepreneurs bring along the diversification in ethnicities, lifestyles and contribute to work creation, production and income.
